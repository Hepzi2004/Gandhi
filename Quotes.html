QUOTES OF MAHATMA GANDHI

Mahatma Gandhi, known for his wisdom and commitment to nonviolence, left behind a treasure trove of inspirational quotes.Here are some of his most famous quotes:


Quote 1: You must be the change you want to see in the world.

Quote 2: An eye for an eye only ends up making the whole world blind.

Quote 3: The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.

Quote 4: The weak can never forgive. Forgiveness is the attribute of the strong.

Quote 5: Happiness is when what you think, what you say, and what you do are in harmony.

Quote 6: In a gentle way, you can shake the world.

Quote 7: The future depends on what you do today.

Quote 8: The greatness of a nation can be judged by the way its animals are treated.

Quote 9: Freedom is not worth having if it does not include the freedom to make mistakes.

Quote 10: You may never know what results come of your actions, but if you do nothing, there will be no results.

Quote 11: Nonviolence is a weapon of the strong.

Quote 12: First, they ignore you, then they laugh at you, then they fight you, then you win.

Quote 13: Earth provides enough to satisfy every man's needs, but not every man's greed.

Quote 14: A small body of determined spirits fired by an unquenchable faith in their mission can alter the course of history.

Quote 15: You can chain me, you can torture me, you can even destroy this body, but you will never imprison my mind.
